\# Privacy Policy for Quizzer Pro



\*\*Effective Date:\*\* November 29, 2025



---



\## 1. Introduction



Quizzer Pro is a \*\*local, offline study tool\*\* designed to help users learn and review questions using spaced repetition. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your data.



---



\## 2. No Data Collection



\- \*\*No Personal Data\*\*: Quizzer Pro does \*\*not\*\* collect, store, or transmit any personal information (e.g., name, email, location, or device identifiers).

\- \*\*No Internet Connection\*\*: The app operates \*\*entirely offline\*\* and does not require an internet connection.

\- \*\*No Analytics or Tracking\*\*: We do \*\*not\*\* track or log how you use the app.



---



\## 3. Data Stored Locally



All data is stored \*\*exclusely on your device\*\* and is never sent to external servers. The app stores the following types of data:



| Data Type               | Description                                                                                     | Storage Location               |

|-------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------|

| \*\*Questions and Decks\*\* | Questions, options, answers, and explanations you add or load into the app.                    | `decks/` folder (JSON files)    |

| \*\*Study Progress\*\*      | Your progress, including mastery scores, Leitner boxes, and spaced repetition schedules (SRS). | `session\_state.json`            |

| \*\*App Settings\*\*        | Preferences like session size and mastery thresholds.                                           | `session\_state.json`            |



---



\## 4. How Data Is Used



\- \*\*Questions and Decks\*\*: Used to create and manage your study material. You can save, load, and edit these files manually.

\- \*\*Study Progress\*\*: Used to track your learning progress (e.g., mastery scores, Leitner boxes) and optimize your study sessions.

\- \*\*App Settings\*\*: Used to customize your study experience (e.g., session size).



\*\*All data is used exclusively within the app and is never shared or transmitted outside your device.\*\*



---



\## 5. Data Security



\- \*\*Local Storage\*\*: Your data is stored \*\*only on your device\*\*. We recommend backing up your `decks/` folder and `session\_state.json` file to avoid data loss.

\- \*\*No Encryption\*\*: The app does \*\*not\*\* encrypt your data. If your device is compromised, your study data could be accessed by others.

\- \*\*GitHub Hosting\*\*: If you download Quizzer Pro from GitHub, \*\*no data is transmitted to GitHub or any third party\*\*. GitHub is used solely for hosting the app's source code.



---



\## 6. User Responsibilities



\- \*\*Backup Your Data\*\*: Regularly back up your `decks/` folder and `session\_state.json` file to prevent data loss.

\- \*\*Device Security\*\*: Secure your device to protect your locally stored data.
